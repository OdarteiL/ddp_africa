Regulatory Intelligence

EU DPP delegated acts are still being written. Our regulatory intelligence service ensures you are never caught off guard by a rule change — with monthly briefings and real-time alerts.

Why Regulatory Intelligence Matters

The EU Digital Product Passport framework is not a finished regulation. The core ESPR legislation is in place, but the specific technical requirements — the delegated acts — are still being developed and published for each product category. Requirements that apply to your products today may be updated, expanded, or clarified before your compliance deadline.

For African exporters, this creates a moving target. You cannot simply comply once and forget about it. You need to track every update, understand what it means for your specific products, and adjust your passports and data collection accordingly.

What You Receive

  • Monthly regulatory briefings covering all DPP-relevant EU publications
  • Real-time alerts when delegated acts affecting your product categories are updated
  • Plain-language interpretation — no legal jargon, just what it means for your business
  • Impact assessments: what changes, what stays the same, what you need to do
  • Deadline tracking and advance warning of upcoming compliance milestones
  • Access to our regulatory Q&A service for specific compliance questions

Standalone or Bundled

Regulatory Intelligence is available as a standalone subscription for manufacturers, trade associations, and industry bodies who want to stay informed without committing to the full platform. It is also available bundled with the DPP Platform subscription at a discounted rate.

Trade associations and export promotion agencies can license the service for distribution to their members — contact us for group pricing.

Pricing

Standalone subscription: €1,200–€3,600/year depending on the number of product categories covered. Bundled with the DPP Platform: included at no additional cost for the first year.
