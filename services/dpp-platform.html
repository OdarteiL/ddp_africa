DPP Platform & Passport Generation

Our cloud platform aggregates your product data, generates EU-compliant Digital Product Passports, and provisions QR codes and NFC tags — all in one place.

The Core of Your DPP Compliance

The DPP Platform is the engine that powers your compliance. It connects your suppliers, aggregates product data, validates it against current EU delegated acts, and generates fully compliant Digital Product Passports — complete with QR codes and NFC tags ready to attach to your products before they ship to Europe.

Built specifically for companies without large internal IT or compliance teams, the platform is designed to be operational within 6–10 weeks of onboarding. No enterprise software implementation. No six-month rollout. Just compliance, fast.

Platform Features

  • Supplier data collection portal — accessible via web browser and API
  • Automated DPP generation aligned to current EU delegated acts
  • QR code and NFC tag provisioning and cloud hosting
  • Real-time regulatory update monitoring — passports auto-flag when rules change
  • Compliance dashboard with product-level status and audit-ready export
  • Multi-product and multi-category support in a single account
  • Role-based access for your team, suppliers, and auditors

How It Works

Once onboarded, your suppliers receive access to a simple data submission portal. They enter the required product data — material composition, origin, certifications — and the platform validates it, flags missing fields, and assembles the passport. You review and approve. The passport is published and the QR code is ready to print.

As EU delegated acts are updated, the platform automatically flags which passports need updating and what new data is required. You are never caught off guard by a regulatory change.

Pricing

The platform is available as an annual SaaS subscription, tiered by number of active products and users. Pricing starts at €4,800/year for SMEs with up to 50 active product lines. Volume discounts apply for larger portfolios and white-label partners.
