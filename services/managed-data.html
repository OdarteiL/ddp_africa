Managed Data Services

We act as your dedicated data partner — proactively collecting supplier declarations, verifying data accuracy, and keeping every passport current so you never have to chase a supplier again.

The Problem With Supplier Data

The hardest part of DPP compliance is not the technology — it is the data. Getting accurate, up-to-date information from dozens or hundreds of suppliers across multiple countries is a full-time job. For most African SMEs, that is a job they cannot afford to hire for.

Managed Data Services solves this. We become your data operations team — chasing suppliers, verifying declarations, flagging inconsistencies, and keeping every passport current as your products and supply chains evolve.

What We Do For You

  • Proactive outreach to all your suppliers for required DPP data
  • Structured data collection using standardised EU-compliant templates
  • Verification and quality assurance of all supplier submissions
  • Flagging and resolution of data gaps and inconsistencies
  • Passport updates when products, suppliers, or regulations change
  • Supplier onboarding support and training for your data portal
  • Monthly data health reports showing passport completeness across your portfolio

Who Needs This Service?

Managed Data Services is the right add-on for any business that has more than 20 active suppliers, operates across multiple product categories, or simply does not have internal bandwidth to manage ongoing data collection. It is also essential for businesses in fast-moving supply chains where supplier relationships and product specifications change frequently.

Target gross margin for clients using this service: 60–70% reduction in internal compliance overhead compared to managing data collection in-house.

Pricing

Managed Data Services is available as an add-on to the DPP Platform subscription, priced at €2,400–€6,000/year depending on the number of active products and suppliers. Volume pricing available for large portfolios.
